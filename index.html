Record a testimony
Why does privacy matter to you?
We're building a public archive of video testimonies on surveillance, privacy, and the right to be left alone. Record a two-minute statement directly in your browser — blur your face or cover your eyes with a bar before it ever leaves your device.
Read this first. Submitted videos are uploaded to archive.org and become part of the permanent public record. You can censor your face (blur or eye-bar) and the raw footage never leaves your device — only the processed file is transmitted. That said, censoring is not a guarantee of anonymity. If you are genuinely concerned about being identified — by voice, background, metadata, or context — do not submit a video. Prefer to write instead? Submit a written testimony.
